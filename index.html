__________ __________

Nestled in a quiet street in South Wentworthville, our family owned care centre has a warm, family feel with abundant places to explore, play and learn. NKA Academy is where children feel at home to create big ideas that happen in both play and learning. At NKA Academy we offer exceptional education and care for children, from six weeks of age through to school age. We believe the early years are the foundation for your child and that by providing meaningful experiences, we can cultivate a love for lifelong learning. Our owner/director Aline, has an extensive background in Early Childhood Education with over 15 years experience. She has sent her own children to childcare and consequently has a passion for quality child care and early education. Aline completely understands what it means to be able to send your child to a centre each morning and walk away with the peace of mind that your child is being cared for in a loving and nurturing environment. She believes the best quality childcare is one that simultaneously provides children with loving care and tenderness and also stimulates them to push their boundaries and explore the world around them.
